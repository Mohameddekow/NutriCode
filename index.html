NutriCode

Search nutrition menu for any disease

Anemia

Anemia is a condition in which you lack enough healthy red blood cells to carry adequate oxygen to your body's tissues

Marasmus

Marasmus is a severe form of malnutrition — specifically, protein-energy undernutrition.

Cancer

Cancer is a disease in which some of the body's cells grow uncontrollably and spread to other parts of the body.

Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic (long-lasting) health condition that affects how your body turns food into energy.

Scurvy

a disease caused by a deficiency of vitamin C, characterized by swollen bleeding gums and the opening of previously healed wounds, which particularly affected poorly nourished sailors until the end of the 18th century.

Kwashiorkor

a form of malnutrition caused by protein deficiency in the diet, typically affecting young children in the tropics.

NutriCode is a web system that allows one to search a particular disease and it curates a diet according to what you have searched.
It also suggests the supermarkets near your location where you can buy the foodstuffs and indicates the approximate price of each foodstuff.
